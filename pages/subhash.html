Subhash Chandra Bose

Indian Freedom Fighter | Leader of INA

Welcome to the Legacy of Subhash

A fearless leader who played a key role in India’s independence struggle.

Key Facts

  • Full Name: Subhash Chandra Bose
  • Born: 23 January 1897, Cuttack, Odisha
  • Known as: Netaji
  • Philosophy: Courage, Patriotism, Unity
  • Occupation: Politician, Freedom Fighter

Historical Significance

  • Leader of the Indian National Army (INA) against British rule.
  • Advocated complete independence from British colonialism.
  • Formed alliances with foreign powers to free India.
  • Inspired millions with his call for self-determination and bravery.

Philosophy

  • Believed in direct action and armed struggle for freedom.
  • Promoted national unity above communal differences.
  • Encouraged youth to actively participate in India’s freedom movement.
  • Legacy continues to inspire Indian patriotism and courage.

Memorials & Legacy

  • Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose International Airport in Kolkata named after him.
  • Subhash Chandra Bose Park and statues across India.
  • Remembered for bravery, leadership, and vision for an independent India.
  • INA Martyrs’ Memorial in Moirang honors his army.
